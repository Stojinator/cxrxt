About

CxRxΤ began making music at a young age through parody and experimentation, creating humorous reinterpretations of popular songs while growing up in Macedonia. After immigrating to Germany, he entered the creative scene through livestreaming under the name Stojinator, where freestyle sessions on beats—initially meant as jokes—slowly revealed a raw and chaotic musical instinct. A turning point came when a close friend introduced him to Playboi Carti’s sound, pushing him to take music seriously. Drawing inspiration from artists like Ken Carson, Yeat, Osamason, Juice WRLD, and Playboi Carti, CxRxΤ released his first official track, “Freak,” marking the beginning of his artistic identity. The name CxRxΤ carries a dual meaning. Originating from the phrase cardiac arrest—shortened to Carrest—it evolved into a symbolic acronym: C for Chaos, R for Rebirth, T for Triumph, with the Xs representing voids left to be filled. The name tells a story of being born into chaos, reshaped through rebirth, and ultimately rising in triumph. Sonically and visually, CxRxΤ embraces disorder. He is drawn to dark, aggressive aesthetics—horrorcore, hardcore, gore-influenced imagery—and thrives in the underground, where chaos isn’t avoided but celebrated. His music reflects rebellion, dominance, and transformation, embodied in recurring lines like “fuck the 6,” “royal blood bleeder,” “the dxrk knight,” and “bitch, I’m Carrest.” CxRxΤ is an underground artist pushing toward the top—not by fitting in, but by bleeding chaos into every record.
